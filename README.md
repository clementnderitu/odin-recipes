# Beef Wellington Recipe

This project is a simple HTML page showcasing the recipe for the classic **Beef Wellington**. It is designed to help users understand and replicate the dish by providing step-by-step instructions, a list of ingredients, and tips for success.

## Features

- **Recipe Overview**: Learn the history and key details about Beef Wellington.
- **Ingredients List**: A detailed list of everything you’ll need to prepare the dish.
- **Step-by-Step Instructions**: Follow a clear and concise guide to cook the perfect Beef Wellington.
- **Images**: Visuals to showcase each stage of the recipe.
- **Responsive Design**: Works on both desktop and mobile browsers.

## Recipe Details`

### Ingredients

- 1.5 lbs (700g) beef tenderloin
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- 8 oz (225g) mushrooms, finely chopped
- 1 shallot, finely diced
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 tbsp fresh thyme leaves
- 6 slices prosciutto
- 1 sheet puff pastry
- 2 tbsp Dijon mustard
- 1 egg (for egg wash)
- Salt and pepper to taste

### Instructions

1. **Prepare the Beef**: Sear the beef tenderloin in olive oil until browned on all sides. Season with salt and pepper, then let it cool.
2. **Make the Mushroom Duxelles**: Sauté mushrooms, shallots, garlic, and thyme until most of the moisture is gone.
3. **Wrap in Prosciutto**: Lay out the prosciutto, spread the mushroom duxelles over it, and wrap around the cooled beef.
4. **Encase in Puff Pastry**: Roll out the puff pastry, place the wrapped beef on top, and seal the edges.
5. **Bake**: Brush with egg wash and bake at 400°F (200°C) for 30-40 minutes, or until golden brown.
6. **Rest and Serve**: Let rest for 10 minutes before slicing.
