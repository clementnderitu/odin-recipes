Beef Wellington Recipe

This project is a simple HTML page showcasing the recipe for the classic Beef Wellington.

Ingredients

  • 1.5 lbs (700g) beef tenderloin
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 8 oz (225g) mushrooms, finely chopped
  • 1 shallot, finely diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tbsp fresh thyme leaves
  • 6 slices prosciutto
  • 1 sheet puff pastry
  • 2 tbsp Dijon mustard
  • 1 egg (for egg wash)
  • salt and pepper to taste

Tips for Success

  • Use a meat thermometer to ensure the beef is cooked to your desired doneness.
  • Chill the wrapped beef before encasing it in puff pastry to keep the layers distinct.
  • Serve with a side of roasted vegetables or a creamy mushroom sauce for extra flavor.
